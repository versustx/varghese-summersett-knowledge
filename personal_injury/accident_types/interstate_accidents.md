# Interstate Accidents

This node represents the core legal knowledge for **Interstate Accidents** personal injury claims in Texas.

## Overview
Victims of Interstate Accidents often face significant physical, emotional, and financial burdens. Texas law allows injured parties to seek compensation for medical bills, lost wages, pain and suffering, and other damages if the injury was caused by another party's negligence.
