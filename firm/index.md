# Varghese Summersett Overview

Varghese Summersett is a premier Texas law firm known for its elite attorneys and exceptional results in personal injury, family law, and criminal defense. The firm is led by Founder & Managing Partner [Benson Varghese](/firm/attorneys/benson_varghese.md) and Founding Partner [Anna Summersett](/firm/attorneys/anna_summersett.md).
