# Letty Martinez | Criminal Defense Attorney

Letty Martinez has over 20 years of prosecutorial experience with the Tarrant County District Attorney's Office, serving as Chief of the Family Violence Unit and Crimes Against Children Unit, and Deputy Chief of the Misdemeanor Division. She also worked as an Assistant U.S. Attorney for the Northern District of Texas and was appointed to the Child Fatality Review Team Committee. She is an experienced trial attorney, having tried over 100 cases.
