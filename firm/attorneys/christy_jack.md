# Christy Jack | Partner

Christy Jack is a nationally-recognized attorney who has tried more than 200 criminal cases before a jury. Prior to becoming a criminal defense attorney, Christy was a prosecutor at the Tarrant County District Attorney's Office for more than two decades, working through the ranks to become a chief prosecutor for 15 years and finishing as a deputy chief and litigation expert. She was appointed to the Governing Board of Texas Civil Commitment Office in 2011, and later became its chair, a position she holds today.
