# Benson Varghese | Founder & Managing Partner

Benson Varghese is the founder and managing partner of Varghese Summersett, a premier Texas law firm known for its elite attorneys and exceptional results in personal injury, family law, and criminal defense. In addition to overseeing the firm — which has offices in Fort Worth, Dallas, Southlake, and Houston — Benson is a seasoned trial attorney and a skilled negotiator dedicated to helping people through life's greatest challenges.
Benson began his legal career as a criminal defense attorney and is **Board Certified in Criminal Law** — the highest designation an attorney can reach. A former prosecutor, he has tried more than 100 state and federal criminal cases before Texas juries, ranging from DWI to murder. Today, while he continues to maintain his criminal defense expertise, Benson focuses primarily on firm growth and personal injury cases, particularly serious matters that may go to trial.
