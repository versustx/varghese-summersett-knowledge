# Tiffany Burks | Partner

Tiffany Burks is a highly-regarded criminal lawyer with over 24 years of trial experience. She served as a prosecutor for 24 years, including 22 years in the Tarrant County District Attorney's Office, retiring as a Deputy Chief prosecutor. She has handled thousands of cases from misdemeanors to murders and supervises associate attorneys at Varghese Summersett.
