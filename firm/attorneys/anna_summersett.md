# Anna Summersett | Founding Partner

Anna Summersett is a founding partner at Varghese Summersett, where she helps manage the business side of the firm. She is also a **Board Certified Criminal Law Specialist** and an experienced trial lawyer. Anna has handled thousands of criminal cases and tried more than 100 cases before Texas juries, ranging from DWI to capital murder. Prior to going into private practice as a criminal defense attorney, Anna served as a prosecutor for the Tarrant County Criminal District Attorney's Office and the Cameron County District Attorney's Office.
