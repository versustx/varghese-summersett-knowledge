# Trafficking of Persons

This node represents the core legal knowledge for **Trafficking of Persons** within the federal criminal justice system.

## Overview
Federal charges and procedures differ significantly from state courts. Federal prosecutors (AUSA) have immense resources, and federal sentencing is governed by the United States Sentencing Guidelines (USSG).
